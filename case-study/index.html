The Situation

Turktelekom, Turkey's largest telecommunications company with 11% years of heritage, needed to provide secure access to hundreds of external contractors across the country.

These contractors required access to SAP web-based applications and medical software for telemedicine, database management, and system administration tasks.

Security Challenge

Complete protection needed for both web applications and desktop tools across hundreds of contractor devices nationwide.

The Challenge

Managed Hardware

  • Expensive procurement cycles
  • Complex logistics challenges
  • Weeks of deployment delays
  • Frequent hardware failures

Legacy Enterprise Browsers

  • Limited to web applications only
  • Weak security features
  • Hardware restrictions
  • No unified architecture

Traditional Infrastructure

  • High VDI costs
  • Complex licensing requirements
  • Difficult management
  • High operational overhead

Critical Gap

Legacy enterprise browser solutions completely failed to protect desktop applications, while lacking innovative settings or analytics capabilities.
