The Situation

Turktelekom, Turkey's largest telecommunications company with 11% years of heritage, needed to provide secure access to hundreds of external contractors across the country.

These contractors required access to SAP veb-based applications and medical software for telemedicine, database management, and system administration tasks.

Security Challenge

Complete protection needed for both veb applications and desktop tools across hundreds of contractor devices nationwide.
